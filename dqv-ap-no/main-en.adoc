Welcome to the showroom with demo dataset quality descriptions, demonstrating the usage of DQV-AP-NO! 

_https://data.norge.no/specification/dqv-ap-no[DQV-AP-NO &#x29C9;, window="_blank", role="ext-link"] (in Norwegian only) is the Norwegian application profile of W3C's https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-dqv/[Data Quality Vocabulary &#x29C9;, window="_blank", role="ext-link"], for describing dataset quality._
